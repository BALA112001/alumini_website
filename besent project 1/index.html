    Welcome to the Alumni Association of Government College of Engineering
    Bodinayakkanur - AAGCEBODI :

    This web portal is a consolidator of all the alumnus who had passed out from our college. Over the years, serious efforts had been put forth by various individuals to bring all alumnus into one forum. And finally it has happened in 2020. This web portal is built as a collaboration platform through which an alumni can interact with fellow alumnus, share valuable information which would benefit others, post the job requirements, mentor and promote networking among the alumni. We had classified the membership as Life time member, donor member and associate member. Membership will expand or limit access to the modules in this portal and other benefits that can be availed in AAGCEBODI. Alumnus can become a member by paying a nominal one time fee which will be added to AAGCEBODI corpus fund. Do join this platform and encourage your batchmates also to join. Let us utilize this platform in best possible manner to expand GCE Alumnus network, so that it helps alumnus and their community.
